Support

If you have trouble with McMinos you are at the right address here. If you had some weird crash, please post your problems in the crashes blog page or contact us directly and we will try to provide you with help as fast as possible.

If you got stuck in the game, we suggest to go to our facebook-page (http://facebook.com/mcminosgame) and discuss the best ways to solve the respective level there. Don't worry, we are hanging out there too and are looking forward to give hints.
